Short Description

So Fresh Saturdays was developed to educate, empower and provide a "safe space" for youth and residents the Englewood community and citywide. So Fresh Saturdays will host youth and adults events that embrace art, culture, community and history.

Company Overview

"So Fresh Saturdays" was develop by a group of Englewood residents who wanted to not only provide a "safe space" for the youth but to also encompass a monthly film workshop series showcasing educational documentaries, focused on culture, community, and history with a follow-up dialogue amongst participants. "So Fresh Saturdays" series also connects our youths will successful black owned businesses in the art industry and learning techniques to promote positive media stories about Englewood in an effort to enhance the imagery of the community.

Mission

Our mission is to educate and elevate the youth in Englewood by using diverse methods of engagement (i.e film, spoken word, music, art etc.).
